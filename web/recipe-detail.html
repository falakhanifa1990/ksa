Crispy Air Fryer Chicken Wings

Perfectly crispy chicken wings made in the air fryer with minimal oil and maximum flavor. These wings are a healthier alternative to deep-fried versions but taste just as delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pat dry the chicken wings with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the wings with olive oil until evenly coated.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together all the dry spices (garlic powder, paprika, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and cayenne if using).
  4. Sprinkle the spice mixture over the wings and toss until evenly coated.
  5. Preheat your air fryer to 380¬∞F (193¬∞C) for 3 minutes.
  6. Place the wings in the air fryer basket in a single layer, making sure they aren't touching (cook in batches if necessary).
  7. Cook for 12 minutes, then flip the wings and cook for another 12 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Check that the internal temperature has reached 165¬∞F (74¬∞C) using a meat thermometer.
  9. Serve immediately while hot, with your favorite dipping sauce.
