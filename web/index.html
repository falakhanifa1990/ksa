Crispy Air Fryer Chicken Wings
Perfectly crispy chicken wings made in the air fryer with minimal oil and maximum flavor.
30 minutes 4 servings Easy 380°F

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings, separated at joints
  • 1 tbsp baking powder (aluminum-free)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
